If raw data already exists, either produced by the FTPC slow simulator (St_fss_Maker) or from a daq data import, StFtpcClusterMaker will immediately invoke the clusterfinder StFtpcClusterFinder . Otherwise, the FTPC fast simulator StFtpcFastSimu will be invoked to generate hits from geant data.
