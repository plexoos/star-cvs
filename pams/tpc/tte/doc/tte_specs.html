TTE SPECS

ModuleFunctional Description
tte.FTracking evaluation package. Based on hit assignment to tracks and the tcl_tpc_index.idl table estimates tracking efficiency, tracking purity, momentum resolution and other aspects of track quality.
tte_track.FUsing information in the tcl_tpc_index.idl table, re-assemble tracks, fit momenta and filll the tpt_track.idl table.Useful in assesing impact of track reconstraction on physics analysis. Provides "upper limit" performance estimate (you cannot beat that).
