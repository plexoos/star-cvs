Meaning of  the word "flag" filled by tfs

  • Hits with flag>1 will be removed by tfs_filt module.
  • During the merging process the word flag contains -id of a hit  the current one should be merged with.
  • A hit that is a product of merging  has flag set to 1.
  • Hits that got outside of the TPC fiducial volume during the pile-up processing are marked with 9.
  • Hits from 1KeV  tracks are marked with 3.
  • Single hits larger than 20cm in both z and along the padrow are marked with 12.
  • Hits that were merged with others and should be deleted from the final lineup are flagged with 8.
  • Hits that in the process of merging became larger than 20cm in both directions are marked with 11.
  • Hits that came from geant hits with de<0 are marked with 5.
