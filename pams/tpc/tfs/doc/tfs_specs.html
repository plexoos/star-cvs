TFS SPECS

ModuleFunctional Description
tfs_g2t.FMain routine.
tfs_ini.FInitialize and load constants and parameters.
tfs_g2t_hit_rd.FLoad Geant hits into TPC hit table. Also loads the TPC index tables.
tfs_hit_sort.FSort hits according to the encoded sector-row information. Sets up a local pointer index to the first and last hits in a given row.
tfs_gen_padres.FAssigns a spatial width and a spatial resolution to the TPC space-points gotten from GEANT. The spatial width (aka the pad response) and the pad resolution are a function of the drift length, pad crossing angle and the dip angle. The parameterizations used here are described in STAR notes (#33 and #34). The values of the coefficients to these expressions have been determined from EOS and ALEPH measurements. These are contained in the parameter table.
tfs_hit_smear.FSmears TPC hit coordinates in pad-row and drift direction according to a Gaussian distribution with its sigma equal to respective spatial resolutions.
tfs_displ_event.F Displaces separate events along the beam direction according to the current bunch crossing number ix. This routine is used to overlap multiple pp events in the high luminosity mode of pp running at RHIC. The average number of events per crossing is calculated in TFS_INI from the beam parameter table.
tfs_tpchit_merge.FMerges TPC hits that are close in pad-row and drift direction.
tfs_filt.cFilter out bad hits and reset the hit counter to the total number of valid TPC hits.
